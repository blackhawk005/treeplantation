ABOUT US

Treeasure stands for tree which stands for plantation, asure sounds like assure that is assuring the growth of plants, and when combined it becomes a treasure with an E in the middle which stands for environment.

Who are we?

We the NSS unit of SIES GST, Nerul have created a website, treeasure to help people plant trees and spread greenery in the easiest way possible. Our vision is to create a green planet where we breathe freshness and keep our mother earth cool. We ensure that you are provided with all the information about how to plant, necessities for the particular plant that you've chosen, available locations for plantation and also links to buy plants online. This way our website makes the work super easy for all. They say 1 step makes a huge difference. So why not we all take 1 step towards a better tomorrow?

About TreeAsure

The website contains information (size, climate, soil, watering/care, uses) of some popular indoor and outdoor plants along with the information of some gardening tools which will be useful during plantation. On this website user can create an event for tree plantation and/or register for these events. User can also buy saplings from some popular online nursing shops whose links are given in the site. Users can also find some locations available for tree plantation uploaded by other users along with the location link (from google maps) and contact information of certain authorities if needed.

Features

-anyone can create event or register for an event (plantation drive).
-one can find available location for tree plantation.
- can buy saplings from link provided of some popular online nursing shops.
- detailed information like size, suitable climate, care and watering of indoor/outdoor plants - information of some basic gardening tools

Advantages

-In the process of making our surroundings filled with greenery, many people start the fun process of tree/seed plantation, but the process of gathering information and knowledge about different plants makes them lose interest in this beautiful hobby. We shorten this process for you by providing only the required information in a very efficient way.
-Our site continues to encourage the users to not only gain more knowledge about all the different plants available, but also helps you to be a part of a community by participating in different plantation drives organized by different people at different locations.
-We take you through the whole experience starting from gaining knowledge about different plants to physically planting them with your own hands.
