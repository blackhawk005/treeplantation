AJUGA

SIZE

Ajuga are matt forming evergreen plants that are ideal as a ground covering plant. Bugleweed has a mass of blue and purple flowering stems that sit above a foliage of leaves, these can be as variable in colour as deep green, purple, pink and cream. Their height ranges from 2-20 inches

CLIMATE

Best time to sow is spring or autumn. These plants can grow in full sunshine or in shade. There is no specific temperature for this plant but for the best growth, 10-12 OC would be the best. Ajuga ground cover spreads through runners.

SOIL

The soil should be acidic with a pH in the range of 3.7 to 6.5. Ajuga prefers moist soil, but will tolerate drought remarkably well.
