CHILLY

SIZE

They grow from small to medium sized bushes from knee high to two metres/six feet tall. How big they get depends on the species and variety.

LIFE CYCLE

Chilies can live between 1.5 - 15 years depending on the species.

CLIMATE

We need a warm climate and sunny position. 18 - 21 OC is the ideal temperature.

SOIL

Requires moisture-retentive soil (clay soil so that more water is retained).

WATERING/CARE

  • Chillies cannot withstand a lot of water. Hence, heavy rain and stagnated water could spoil your plant. Make gaps in your soil and holes at the bottom of your pot to make sure it is not over-watered.
  • Give at least 2 inches of water per week once the fruits have appeared, spray rather than water at the base to prevent over watering.
  • Pinch out growing tips when plants reach about 15-20cm (6-8in) high to encourage bushy growth and better cropping.
