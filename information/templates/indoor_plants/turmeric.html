TURMERIC PLANT

SIZE

Turmeric plants reach about 1 metre (3.3 feet) in height and bear long simple leaves with long petioles (leaf stems). The leaves emerge from the branching rhizomes that lie just below the soil surface.

LIFE-CYCLE

Turmeric takes seven to 10 months from planting to harvest.

CLIMATE

Temperatures between 20 and 30 °C (68 and 86 °F) and a considerable amount of annual rainfall

SOIL REQUIREMENTS

Turmeric thrives in warm, humid conditions and well-drained, neutral soil

WATERING/CARE

Water it about every two days, and spray it with a mist bottle between waterings.
