Pruning Saw

A pruning saw is a tool with the same sharp teeth as saws used for cutting lumber. But pruning saws are intended to help you trim larger shrubs and smaller tree branches.

When to use?

Shrubs and trees will need shaping and pruning from time to time, not to mention those woody weeds like briars that will need to be tackled too.
